Ingredients:
 - 2 T olive oil
 - 12 tomatoes, chopped
 - 1  onion, chopped
 - 10 cloves garlic, chopped
 - One ~102 -ounce can diced tomato
 - fresh basil
 - 1 qt heavy cream
 - salt to taste

Directions:
 1. Heat oil in pot
 2. Add fresh tomatoes and onion and cook on med high for about 5 min.
 3. Add garlic and cook another 30 sec.
 4. Add diced tomato. Reduce heat and simmer about 15 min.
 5. Remove from heat. Add basil and blend.
 6. Add cream and salt to taste. return to heat to bring to temp.
